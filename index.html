Informatics Teaching Guide

Andrew J. Ko, Ph.D.

Teaching is hard, especially in higher education, where few people have a formal training in learning, teaching, or education. I wrote this book to support the many teachers at the University of Washington who lack training in teaching, providing a personal, but also evidence-based understanding of teaching and learning. It's targeted to teachers in the Informatics program, but likely valuable to other teachers in higher education.

I hope you enjoy! If not, please file an issue on the the book's GitHub repository.
